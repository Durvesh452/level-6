# TrustLand Demo Day Presentation

## Slide 1: The Problem
**Land Fraud: A Global Crisis**
- Every year, thousands of landowners lose their properties to forged documents and corrupt registries.
- Traditional registries are opaque and vulnerable to tampering.
- **Goal**: Create an immutable "Source of Truth" for land documents.

## Slide 2: The Solution
**TrustLand: Decentralized Land Integrity**
- We anchor land documents to the **Stellar Blockchain**.
- Using SHA-256 hashing, we ensure documents cannot be modified without detection.
- A seamless, professional experience for both owners and authorities.

## Slide 3: Live Demo (Key Features)
1.  **Gasless Experience**: No XLM? No problem. Our **Fee Sponsorship** covers your costs.
2.  **Instant Verification**: Verify any document against the global ledger in seconds.
3.  **Onboarding**: Multi-step, secure identity setup.

## Slide 4: Under the Hood (Tech Stack)
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Blockchain**: Stellar Network (Fee Bump Transactions)
- **Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL) for real-time indexing.
- **Monitoring**: Sentry & Structured JSON Logging.

## Slide 5: Traction & Proof
- **30+ Active Testnet Users**: Verifiable on-chain.
- **Production Ready**: Full security suite (Rate limiting, Zod, Sanitization).
- **Scalable**: Built for 30+ users and ready for mass adoption.

## Slide 6: The Roadmap
