# TrustLand User Guide

Welcome to TrustLand! This guide will help you secure your property documents and verify their authenticity on the blockchain.

## 1. Getting Started

### Step 1: Create an Account
1. Click **Get Started** on the homepage.
2. Enter your email and create a secure password.
3. TrustLand will generate a unique **Stellar Blockchain ID** for you.
4. **IMPORTANT**: Securely save your **Secret Key**. TrustLand does not store it and cannot recover it if lost.

### Step 2: Onboarding
1. Enter your full name and confirm your wallet address.
2. Generate your unique **Referral Link** to invite others.

## 2. Registering a Document

1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click **Register New Document**.
3. Drag and drop your document (PDF, Image, etc.).
4. Enter property details (Name, Location, Survey ID).
5. Click **Anchoring to Blockchain**.
6. **Gasless**: You don't need XLM to pay for fees; TrustLand sponsors your first 100 transactions!

## 3. Verifying a Document

1. Go to the **Verify** page.
2. Upload the document you wish to check.
3. Click **Verify Against Ledger**.
4. The system will tell you instantly if the document is authentic and show the original registration details.

## 4. Managing Your Portfolio

- View all your registered documents in the **Dashboard**.
- Click the **Explorer** link to see the raw blockchain record on Stellar Expert.
- Check your wallet balance (though fees are sponsored, you can still hold XLM).

## 5. FAQ

**Q: Does TrustLand store my documents?**
A: No. We only store the cryptographic hash. Your actual files never leave your device.

**Q: What happens if I modify my document?**
A: Even a single character change will result in a different hash. The verification tool will show "Verification Failed," protecting you from tampering.

**Q: Is there a fee?**
A: Currently, all transactions are sponsored by TrustLand on the testnet. No cost to you!
